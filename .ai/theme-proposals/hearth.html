§ The method

Why twice, really.

The first bake is the code. Anyone with a keyboard can write code — it's the second bake that matters: sitting with the software beside you, tasting it, adjusting the seasoning, noticing what you actually reach for at 9am on a Tuesday.

Every engagement we take includes a second pass by default. A month or two after the first build, we come back with fresh eyes, a list of what the software asked for while it was being used, and time set aside to answer it.

We keep our client list small on purpose. It lets us know your business well enough to give real advice — not just ship tickets — and most of our work comes from people we've worked with for years.
